## Project 1 : Cyclistic bike share

<p> As a data analyst in the marketing analyst team, the objective is to develop a new marketing strategy to convert casual riders into annual members. The study follows the data analysis process, encompasses the steps of asking relevant questions, preparing the data, processing and analyzing it, sharing insights, and suggesting actionable recommendations.
This article specifically addresses the initial question of understanding the differences between annual members and casual riders. By leveraging data analytics techniques, the study aims to uncover key insights that can inform targeted marketing tactics and drive the conversion of casual riders into annual member. </p>
